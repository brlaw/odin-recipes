Hot Chocolate

Description

Hot chocolate is a delicious, comforting beverage made by mixing milk (or water) with cocoa powder and sugar, and heating it until smooth and creamy. It's the perfect drink to warm you up on a cold day or enjoy as a sweet treat any time.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk (or water for a lighter version)
  • 2 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract (optional)
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. In a small pot, combine the cocoa powder and sugar.
  2. Pour in a small amount of milk (about 2 tablespoons) and stir until you form a smooth paste.
  3. Gradually add the rest of the milk while stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  4. Place the pot over medium heat and stir occasionally until the mixture is hot but not boiling.
  5. If desired, stir in vanilla extract for extra flavor.
  6. Once the hot chocolate is smooth and heated through, pour it into a mug.
  7. Top with whipped cream or marshmallows, if desired. Enjoy!
